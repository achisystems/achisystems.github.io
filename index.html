Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a website cost in Nairobi?
Website costs in Nairobi range from KES 25,000 for a basic website to KES 100,000+ for complex e-commerce solutions. The price depends on features, design complexity, and functionality requirements.
How long does it take to build a website?
A basic website takes 1-2 weeks, while complex websites can take 4-8 weeks. Timeline depends on content preparation, revisions, and feature complexity.
Do you provide website maintenance services?
Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages including security updates, content updates, backups, and technical support starting from KES 5,000 per month.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops to ensure the best user experience across all platforms.
Do you offer SEO services in Nairobi?
Yes, we provide comprehensive SEO services including keyword research, on-page optimization, content strategy, and local SEO to help your business rank higher on Google.
Can you redesign my existing website?
Yes, we specialize in website redesigns. We can improve your current website's design, functionality, and performance while maintaining your existing content and SEO rankings.
Do you create e-commerce websites?
Yes, we build complete e-commerce solutions with product catalogs, shopping carts, payment gateways (M-Pesa, card payments), and inventory management systems.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, M-Pesa payments, and cash payments. We require a 50% deposit to start the project and the remaining 50% upon completion.
Do you provide hosting and domain services?
Yes, we provide reliable web hosting services and can help you register and manage your domain name. We also offer website migration services.
Can I update my website content myself?
Yes, we build websites with user-friendly content management systems (CMS) that allow you to easily update text, images, and other content without technical knowledge.
